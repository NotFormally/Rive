# RIVE — Demo Video Script
### "Your Restaurant, Algorithmically Served."

**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover + text overlays
**Tone:** Cinematic, premium, confident — think Apple product launch meets fine dining
**Target:** Restaurant owners & executive chefs (potential clients)
**Estimated Runtime:** 5–7 minutes
**Music:** Minimal electronic ambient, slow build — think Ólafur Arnalds meets Bonobo

---

## PRE-PRODUCTION NOTES

### Recording Setup
- **Tool:** Screen Studio (macOS) for auto-zoom & smooth cursor — or OBS + post-edit
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840×2160), export at 1080p
- **Browser:** Arc or Chrome, fullscreen, no bookmarks bar
- **Cursor:** Hide system cursor, use Screen Studio smooth cursor
- **Speed:** Record at natural pace, speed up transitions in post (1.5×)
- **Dashboard data:** Seed the database with realistic French fine dining data before recording (menu items, reservations, ingredients, recipes, logbook entries)

### Data Seeding Checklist (Before Recording)
- [ ] Restaurant profile: "Le Comptoir de Verre" (or similar elegant name)
- [ ] 15–20 menu items across 4 categories (Entrées, Plats, Desserts, Boissons)
- [ ] 8–10 ingredients with realistic unit costs
- [ ] 3–4 complete recipes linked to menu items
- [ ] 5–6 logbook entries (varied languages, sentiments)
- [ ] 10+ reservations (mix of statuses)
- [ ] 2–3 scanned invoices
- [ ] Smart Prep data populated from reservations + POS
- [ ] Intelligence Score at ~65% ("Predictive" milestone)

## POST-PRODUCTION NOTES

### Text Overlays Style
- Font: Jakarta Sans (bold) for headlines, Outfit for body
- Color: #F2F0E9 (cream) on dark backgrounds, #1A1A1A on light
- Accent: #CC5833 for emphasis words
- Animation: Fade up + slight blur resolve (0.4s)
- Position: Bottom-left or center depending on context

### Transitions
- Between scenes: Quick fade to black (0.3s) or direct cut
- Within scenes: Smooth scroll / pan (Screen Studio auto-zoom)
- Speed ramps: 1× for feature showcases, 1.5× for navigation transitions

### Color Grading
- Slight warm grade to match Rive brand (amber shadows)
- Increase contrast slightly on dashboard screenshots
- Vignette on close-up shots

### Subtitles
- Provide EN and FR subtitle tracks (SRT files)
- Style: White text with slight dark shadow, bottom center

### Music Licensing Suggestions
- Artlist.io or Epidemic Sound
- Search: "minimal electronic ambient", "cinematic tech", "luxury brand"
- Avoid: Anything with a beat drop, anything too "startup-y"
- Reference tracks: Ólafur Arnalds "Near Light", Bonobo "Cirrus"
